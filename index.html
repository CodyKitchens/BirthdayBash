Greetings, Brave Souls,

As I prepare to bid a fiery farewell to my roaring 30s, I invite you to join me for a day of intrigue and a night of remembrance. Our adventure begins with a daring Mafia-style water gun battle, where alliances are tested and only the cunning will survive. As dusk falls, gather around as we ignite the night with a Viking funeral to honor the decade past.

  • 12:00 PM: Mafia-style Water Gun Battle Begins
  • 6:00 PM: Viking Funeral Ceremony & Ship Burning
  • 9:00 PM: Cigars, Camaraderie, and Poker Tournament

Come clad in your best Viking or funeral attire to partake in the ceremonies, share a roast or eulogy in good spirits, and laugh at the memories we've forged. After we pay our respects, the night will conclude with cigars, camaraderie, and a poker tournament under the stars.

Note: Let us relive the glory days and send off the 30s with a proper Viking goodbye, unburdened by the watchful eyes of the young.

Your presence is both an honor and a boon. Please RSVP by April 11th, 2025 to secure your place in the shield wall or at the poker table.
